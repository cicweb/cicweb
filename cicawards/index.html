About CICAwards™ 2025

Community Recognition

The CICAwards™ Edition 2025 celebrates the amazing individuals in our WhatsApp community who consistently contribute, support others, and create a vibrant environment.

Award Categories

We have multiple award categories to recognize different contributions - from most helpful member to most creative content creator, and everything in between.

Voting Process

Some awards will be chosen through community polls where all members can vote, while others will be selected by our admin team based on consistent contributions.

Award Rules & Guidelines

One Award Per Member

No member can receive more than one award in this edition of CICAwards™. This ensures fair recognition across our diverse community.

Unique Award Recipients

Each award category can only be given to one member. Awards are not shared between multiple recipients to maintain the special recognition value.

Selection Process

Some awards will be chosen by community members through polls, while others will be selected by the Admin team based on contributions and impact.

Fair Play Policy

Any attempts to manipulate voting or influence the selection process unfairly will result in disqualification from award consideration.
