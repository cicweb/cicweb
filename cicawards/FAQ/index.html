Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 When is CICAwards™?

+

A1: The CICAwards™ 2025 ceremony will take place on December 20th, 2025 at 18:00 (Germany Time).

You can check the countdown on our official website: ziadgo.uk/cicawards

Q2 Can I use another number to vote for my favorite member/admin?

+

A2: No, you cannot use another number to vote for your favorite member or admin. This is strictly against the guidelines.

If you are caught using multiple numbers to vote:

  • You will be immediately disqualified from voting
  • You will receive an official warning
  • Depending on your number of previous warnings, you may be banned from the community

We maintain fair voting practices to ensure the integrity of the awards.

Q3 Why am I not on the best member poll?

+

A3: There are specific criteria for being included in the best member poll:

  1. Activity Requirement: You must have been active in the community for the last 60 days prior to the awards
  2. Engagement Level: You might not have sent enough messages or interacted sufficiently with other members

We evaluate both the duration of activity and the quality/quantity of engagement when selecting members for polls.

Q4 Will I get an award?

+

A4: Award selection depends on several factors:

  1. Admin Selection: Some awards are chosen by the admin team based on contributions and community impact
  2. Community Polls: Other awards are determined by community voting

We cannot disclose any award winners before the ceremony. The entire purpose of hosting CICAwards™ is to announce the awards during the event. We maintain confidentiality to ensure excitement and fairness for all participants.

This approach is standard practice for any awards ceremony to maintain integrity and surprise.
