Award Rules & Guidelines

1 One Award Per Member

No single member can receive more than one award in this edition of CICAwards‚Ñ¢. This ensures fair recognition across our diverse community and allows more members to be celebrated.

2 Unique Award Recipients

Each award category can only be given to one member. Awards are not shared between multiple recipients to maintain the special recognition value of each category.

3 Selection Process

Awards will be selected through two methods: some by community member polls and others by Admin team selection based on contributions and impact within the community.

4 Fair Play Policy

Any attempts to manipulate voting or influence the selection process unfairly will result in immediate disqualification from award consideration.

5 Community Standards

All nominees must be in good standing with the community guidelines and have no recent violations of community rules to be eligible for awards.

6 Active Membership

To be eligible for awards, members must have been active participants in the WhatsApp community for at least 60 days prior to the award nominations.
