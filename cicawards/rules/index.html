Detailed Rules & Guidelines

One Award Per Member

No single member can receive more than one award in this edition of CICAwards‚Ñ¢. This ensures fair recognition across our diverse community and allows more members to be celebrated for their unique contributions.

Unique Award Recipients

Each award category can only be given to one member. Awards are not shared between multiple recipients to maintain the special recognition value of each category and ensure that every award winner receives exclusive recognition.

Selection Process

Awards will be selected through two methods: some by community member polls and others by Admin team selection based on contributions and impact within the community. The selection method for each category will be clearly communicated.

Fair Play Policy

Any attempts to manipulate voting or influence the selection process unfairly will result in immediate disqualification from award consideration. This includes but is not limited to vote manipulation, fake accounts, or any form of cheating.

Community Standards

All nominees must be in good standing with the community guidelines and have no recent violations of community rules to be eligible for awards. Members with disciplinary actions within the last 90 days will not be considered.

Active Membership

To be eligible for awards, members must have been active participants in the WhatsApp community for at least 60 days prior to the award nominations. Activity is measured by regular participation and positive engagement.
